Sports Material

Here's the basics of several sports.

Running

Running is one of the most popular aerobic exercise. It helps to improve cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and enhances endurance. Start with a warm-up, and gradually increase your distance and speed.

Benefits of Running:

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Boosts lung capacity
  • Strengthens muscles, especially in the calf and thighs
  • Help people in weight management

Push-up

Push-up is a great upper body exercise that targets the chest, triceps, and shoulders. It also engages the core muscles, making it being considered as a full body workout. To do a push-up, place your hands slightly wider than your shoulder apart, lower your body to the ground, and push back up to the previous (starting) position.

Benefits of Push-up:

  • Strengthens the chest, shoulders, and triceps
  • Engages core muscles
  • Improves upper body strength

Sit-up

Sit-up is a classic core exercise that targets the abdominal muscles. This exercise helps people to build a strong core muscle, beneficial for a good posture. To perform a sit-up, lie on your back with your knees bent, and lift up your upper body toward your knees.

Benefits of Sit-up:

  • Strengthens abdominal/core muscles
  • Helps improve posture
  • Can be used into a variety of fitness routines

Back-up

Back-up, sometimes called back extensions is an exercise that target the lower back muscles, glutes, and hamstrings. They help people to improve posture and strengthen the muscles that support the spine. To perform a back-up, lie face down on the floor, place your hands behind your head, and lift your chest off the ground.

Benefits of Back-up:

  • Strengthens lower back muscles
  • Improves posture and spinal support
  • Engages glutes and hamstrings
  • Helps prevent lower back pain

Squat

Squat is a fundamental lower body exercise that targets the hamstrings, quads, glutes, and calves. It helps people to build strength and endurance in the legs and improve mobility. To perform a squat, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, bend your knees, and lower your body as if you're sitting back into a chair.

Benefits of Squat:

  • Strengthens legs and glutes
  • Improves balance and flexibility
  • Boosts endurance

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport that combines cardio, endurance, and coordination. Players must dribble, pass, and shoot the basketball to enemy's basket to score points while defending their own basket.

Benefits of Basketball:

  • Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Enhances coordination and agility
  • Boosts strength and endurance
  • Builds teamwork

Futsal

Futsal is a variant of soccer played on a smaller court, typically indoors. It involves five players each team and emphasizes quick passes and agility. Futsal is excellent for developing soccer skills in a more condensed and fast-paced environment.

Benefits of Futsal:

  • Enhances footwork and ball control
  • Improves agility and decision making
  • Increases cardiovascular fitness
